 Techno Fussion
Techno Fussion
Organizers:
 ISTE Office Bearers
IRC Auditorium
AUG 30th to SEP 1st
Techno Fusion 2k23 is an exciting technology event featuring three sub-events that cater to diverse skills and interests.



The first sub-event, Quandary Quest, is a quiz competition spanning three rounds. Round 1 involves general questions, Round 2 is a picture quiz, and Round 3 combines various elements to challenge participants.



The second sub-event, Quest of the Golden Slide, requires participants to create presentations on given topics related to the Internet of Things (IoT). This encourages students to showcase their research, innovative thinking, and presentation skills.



The third sub-event, Code Craft, is a coding competition conducted through HackerRank. This event provides a platform for participants to demonstrate their coding prowess and problem-solving abilities.

IOT Prototypin with Arduino
Organizers:
 Ugandhar
Surya Kiran
Shaila

Embark on a transformative learning journey with our upcoming 5-day workshop on IoT Prototyping with Arduino! This immersive experience is designed to empower participants with hands-on skills in Internet of Things (IoT) technology, using the versatile Arduino platform.

Throughout the workshop, attendees can expect a dynamic blend of live sessions and practical hands-on exercises.

Our expert instructors will guide you through the intricacies of IoT prototyping, providing valuable insights and real-world applications. Engage in live discussions to deepen your understanding and stay connected with the latest industry trends.

The practical sessions will be the heart of the workshop, allowing participants to apply their newfound knowledge in real-time. Work with Arduino boards, sensors, and actuators to bring IoT concepts to life.

By the end of the 5 days, you'll not only grasp the theoretical foundations of IoT but also have the practical skills to develop your own Arduino-based prototypes.

Join us on this exciting journey where theory meets practice, and innovation takes center stage. Secure your spot for an enriching workshop experience that promises to expand your horizons in the world of IoT and Arduino prototyping. Let's build the future together!
